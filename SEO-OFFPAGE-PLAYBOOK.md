## 3. Backlink strategy (earn, don't buy)

1. **Data-PR (best):** turn your own data into citable assets — weekly sector-rotation report, "top gainers we called" recap, win-rate stats. Publish on `/blog`, pitch to newsletters. Each citation = an editorial backlink.
2. **Glossary/tool citations:** the `/learn` glossary is linkable reference material — mention it in community answers where someone asks "what is BTC dominance / flow intensity."
3. **Guest posts / AMAs:** offer a "how to read money flow" guest article to crypto blogs; do a Telegram/Twitter Space AMA.
4. **Partnerships:** cross-links with complementary (non-competing) tools, and the ecosystem logos already in your footer.
5. **Brand entity consistency:** identical name, logo, and URL across X, Telegram, IG, TradingView, YouTube → strengthens the "LuxQuant" entity Google builds (already lifting your branded search).

## 4. Content calendar (velocity = compounding)

Keep a steady cadence — Google rewards freshness + topical depth. Everything auto-prerenders on deploy.

**Weekly (pick 2–3):**
- 1 new **glossary term** (`src/content/glossary.js`) — e.g., open interest, RSI, market cap vs FDV, TVL, staking, gas.
- 1 new **blog post** (`src/content/posts.js`) — see backlog below.
- 2–4 new **coins** (`src/content/coins.js`) as coverage expands.
- 1 **social→content** post: every IG/X/Telegram post links to a public `/learn`, `/blog`, or `/coins` page (feeds SEO + referral).

**Blog backlog (high-intent topics):**
- "Altseason index explained: is it altcoin season?"
- "Crypto risk scoring: how to size a trade"
- "Reading DEX volume vs CEX volume"
- "Stablecoin dominance: the sidelines indicator"
- "How win rate and R:R actually compound"
- Per-narrative deep dives: "Best AI crypto coins to watch", "RWA tokens explained"

**Monthly:**
- Refresh top pages (update dates/stats) — signals freshness.
- Review GSC Performance: double down on queries at position 5–15 (quick wins).

## 5. What to track (GSC + analytics)

1. **Indexed pages** trending up (GSC → Pages).
2. **Non-branded** impressions/clicks (filter out `lux*`) — the real scoreboard.
3. **Referring domains** (GSC → Links) — off-page progress.
4. **Organic → signup** conversion (GA4/Plausible event on the CTA).
5. Rank of target clusters (money flow, BTC dominance, per-coin).

> Cadence beats intensity. 2–3 assets/week + a few links/month, sustained for 3–6 months, is what turns organic into your most reliable acquisition channel.

# Guide: Portfolio

The Portfolio page shows your connected Binance account — balances and open positions
— in one place. This guide explains it.

> Note: This displays your account data. It is not a recommendation to buy or sell.

## What it shows

- **Balances / equity** — your account value and available funds.
- **Futures positions** — each open futures position with:
  - **Symbol** — the pair.
  - **Size** — position amount.
  - **Entry** — your entry price.
  - **PnL** — unrealized profit/loss (green = profit, red = loss).
  - **Leverage** — the multiplier used.
  - **Margin** — isolated or cross.
- **Open positions count** — how many positions are currently open.

## Requirements

Portfolio needs a connected Binance account (API key) to read your balances and
positions. It's a read-only view of your account.

## How to use it

Use it to monitor your live exposure and unrealized PnL at a glance. If it's empty,
check that your Binance connection is set up.

## Important note

This is your account information for monitoring, not financial advice.

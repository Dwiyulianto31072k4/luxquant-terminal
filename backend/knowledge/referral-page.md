# Guide: Referral

The Referral page lets you invite others to LuxQuant and earn rewards when they sign up
and subscribe. It also tracks your referrals and lets you cash out earnings. This guide
explains it.

> Note: This explains the referral program mechanics only.

## What it shows

- **Your referral code / link** — share it to invite people.
- **Funnel** — how your referred users progress through stages:
  - **Signed Up** — created an account.
  - **Active** — using the product.
  - **Subscribed** — became a paying subscriber (this is what earns you commission).
  - **Churned / Cancelled** — lapsed or cancelled.
- **Earnings / commissions** — what you've earned from subscribed referrals.
- **Cashout** — request a payout of your earnings and view your cashout history and its
  status (Pending, etc.).

## How to use it

Share your **referral link**, track your **funnel** to see how many referrals convert
to subscribers, and use **Cashout** to withdraw earnings once you've accrued enough.

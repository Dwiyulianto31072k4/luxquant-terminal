# Guide: Tips

The Tips page is LuxQuant's library of educational content — guides and lessons to help
you use the terminal and trade more effectively. This guide explains it.

> Note: Tips are educational material, not personalized financial advice.

## What it shows

- A collection of **tips / guides**, each with a cover and content (some as readable
  documents / PDFs).
- **Categories** — filter tips by topic to find what you need.
- Open a tip to read the full content.

## How to use it

Browse by category, open the tips relevant to what you're learning (how a feature works,
a trading concept, etc.), and use them alongside the in-app assistant for quick help.

## Note

Tips are for education and general guidance. They are not personalized financial advice.

# Guide: AI Research (BTC Compass)

AI Research is LuxQuant's AI-generated Bitcoin market analysis workspace, powered by
the **BTC Compass**. It turns live market data into a structured "read" of where BTC
stands and where it may head over the next 24 hours and beyond. This guide explains
each part.

> Note: This is analysis and information, not a recommendation to buy or sell. It
> helps you understand the market read; trading decisions are your own.

## The headline read

At the top you'll see the current BTC **direction** and **confidence**:

- **Bullish ↑ / Bearish ↓ / Neutral →** — the AI's directional read.
- **Confidence %** — how strong that read is (higher = more conviction).
- A freshness/health indicator shows when the latest Compass read was generated. Use
  the refresh action to build the newest read.

## Workspace sections

- **The Read** — the core AI summary of the current market: the 24-hour tactical view,
  the direction, and the rationale behind it.
- **Longer View** — the broader / strategic perspective beyond the next 24 hours
  (bigger structure and context).
- **BTC Projection Chart** — live BTC candles (where price trades now) plus the
  Compass projection overlay: magnets (price attractors), zones, target areas, and the
  **invalidation** level where the read breaks. It shows *why* a bullish or bearish
  read points to a specific area.
- **Verdict Ledger** — the track record of past Compass verdicts, so you can see how
  previous reads played out (transparency / accountability of the AI's calls).
- **Brain** — the inputs and reasoning behind the read: the data drivers the AI
  weighed (price action, derivatives, liquidity, etc.) to reach its conclusion.
- **Compass Reports** — full written Compass breakdowns, viewable and downloadable as
  PDF, archived over time.

## Key terms

- **Tactical 24h** — the short-term (next ~24 hours) read and rationale.
- **Strategic / Longer view** — the higher-timeframe context.
- **Invalidation** — the price level at which the current read is considered wrong
  (the thesis breaks).
- **Magnets / zones** — areas price is likely drawn to or reacts around, drawn on the
  projection chart.
- **Confidence** — the strength of the read (a percentage).

## How to use it

- Read **The Read** first for the 24h call and why.
- Check the **Projection Chart** to see the target area and the invalidation level.
- Use the **Verdict Ledger** to judge how reliable recent reads have been.
- Open **Brain** if you want to see what data drove the conclusion.
- The BTC Compass read here is the same market context that informs how to trade the
  signals on the Potential Trades page (e.g. "range only" vs trending conditions).

## Important note

AI Research explains the market and the AI's reasoning. It is not financial advice and
does not tell you to buy or sell — use it as one input for your own decisions.

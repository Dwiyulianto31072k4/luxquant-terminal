# Guide: Home (Overview)

The Home page is your market overview dashboard — the day's top LuxQuant performers,
overall market sentiment, and the biggest movers, all at a glance. This guide explains
it.

> Note: This is a market overview, not a recommendation to buy or sell.

## Sections

- **Top Gainers by LuxQuant** — the best-performing recent signals from the LuxQuant
  algorithm (tap a coin to see its call proof). Time ranges and categories are
  available.
- **Market Overview** — headline market stats like total market cap and 24h volume.
- **Fear & Greed** — the market sentiment gauge (0–100; low = fear, high = greed).
- **Top Gainers / Top Losers (24h)** — the biggest movers in the last 24 hours.

## How to use it

Use Home as your starting point each day: check overall **sentiment** and **market
stats**, scan **Top Gainers by LuxQuant** for standout signals, and jump into Potential
Trades, Bitcoin, or Markets for detail.

## Where to go next

- **Signals** — the full live signal terminal.
- **AI Research** — the BTC Compass market read.
- **Markets / Bitcoin** — broader market and BTC data.

## Important note

The Home overview is informational, not financial advice.

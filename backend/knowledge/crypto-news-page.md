# Guide: News

The News page is a curated feed of crypto news from multiple sources, auto-sorted into
categories so you can quickly find what's relevant. This guide explains it.

> Note: News is context and information, not a recommendation to buy or sell.

## Categories

Articles are auto-tagged by topic:
- **Bitcoin** — BTC-related news.
- **Ethereum** — ETH-related news.
- **Altcoins** — SOL, XRP, ADA, DOGE, TON, and other alts.
- **Macro** — Fed/FOMC, rate cuts, inflation, ETF flows, regulation (SEC/CFTC),
  liquidity — the big-picture drivers.
- **DeFi** — TVL, yield, staking, lending, DEX protocols.
- **Listings** — new exchange listings, token unlocks, airdrops.

## How to use it

Filter by the category you care about, and pay special attention to **Macro** and
**Listings** — those often move prices the most. Each article shows its source; click
to read the full story.

## Important note

News helps you understand what's driving the market. It is not financial advice —
headlines can be noisy and prices may already reflect the news.

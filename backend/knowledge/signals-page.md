# Guide: Potential Trades (Signals Page)

The **Potential Trades** page shows trading signals generated by the LuxQuant
algorithm as a table (the Signals Table). Each row is one signal for one pair
(e.g. BTCUSDT). This document explains how to use it.

## Reading a signal row (columns)

- **Pair** — the coin pair, e.g. `BTCUSDT` (the coin BTC priced in USDT).
- **Price** — the current price of the pair, with its percentage change.
- **Entry** — the suggested entry price for the signal.
- **Target** — take-profit target(s). A signal can have several targets (TP1–TP4).
  Green values show the distance to target from entry.
- **Stop Loss (SL)** — the risk-limit price; if hit, the signal closes at a loss.
- **Risk** — the signal's risk level: `NORMAL` or `HIGH`. HIGH means more volatile /
  higher risk.
- **MCap** — market capitalization (a size indicator; small = small-cap).
- **Vol 24H** — 24-hour trading volume (liquidity).
- **WR / Streak** — **Win Rate**: the historical percentage of signals of this
  pattern that reached target. **Streak**: the recent run of wins/losses
  (e.g. ▲2W = 2 wins in a row, ▼1L = 1 loss).
- **BTC Corr** — correlation to Bitcoin. The ρ (rho) and β (beta) values show how
  closely and how strongly the coin moves with BTC. "Decoupled" = moving
  independently of BTC.
- **Verdict** — a quick Coin Intelligence assessment: `WORTH IT` with a score, or an
  avoid marker.
- **Status** — the signal's current state: `OPEN` (still running), `TP1`/`TP2`/`TP3`/
  `TP4` (nth target hit), `WIN` (closed in profit), `LOSS` (stop loss hit).
- **Called Time** — when the signal was called (created), plus "x hours ago".

## Filtering signals (Call Filter)

- **Date tabs** — Watchlist, All Days, Today, Yesterday, or a specific date. The
  number next to a label is the count of signals for that day.
- **Search pair** — type a coin name to filter. Search is token-based: type `BTC` for
  everything starting with BTC, or `BTCUSDT` for an exact pair match.
- **Called Time / order** — controls sorting (newest/oldest, etc.).
- **Advanced Filters** (expandable section):
  - **Status** — filter by open / updated / outcome.
  - **Risk** — NORMAL or HIGH.
  - **Intelligence Filters** — *Hot streak* (signals with a strong recent win run),
    and **Verdict** (*Worth It* / *Avoid*).
  - **BTC correlation** — *Decoupled* (independent of BTC) or *High align* (moves
    strongly with BTC).
  - **Pattern Filters** — filter by technical pattern tags (e.g. `fvg near entry`,
    `rsi overbought`, `bb expansion`, `vol climax`).
- **Reset All** — clears all active filters.
- **Watchlist** — the star on each row saves a signal to your personal watchlist.

## Finding promising small-cap coins

Combine filters: look for a small **MCap**, make sure **Vol 24H** is healthy
(liquidity), then reinforce with **Verdict = Worth It** and/or **Hot streak**. Also
check **Risk** — small-caps are often `HIGH`.

## Quick glossary

- **TP1–TP4** = Take Profit levels 1 to 4 (staged profit targets).
- **SL / SL1 / SL2** = Stop Loss (risk limits).
- **WR** = Win Rate. **Streak** = the run of wins/losses.
- **FVG** = Fair Value Gap; **RSI** = Relative Strength Index; **BB** = Bollinger Bands.
- **Decoupled** = moving independently of Bitcoin's direction.

## Important note

The signals and data on this page are **tools and information**, not a recommendation
to buy or sell. All trading decisions are the user's own.

# Guide: Watchlist

The Watchlist is your saved list of signals. Star any signal (on the Potential Trades
page or in a signal modal) to add it here and follow it with live prices. This guide
explains it.

> Note: This is a tracking tool, not a recommendation to buy or sell.

## How signals get here

Click the **star** on a signal row or in its detail modal to add it to your watchlist.
Click the star again to remove it. The watchlist shows live prices for the pairs you're
following.

## Filter tabs

- **All** — every saved signal.
- **Open** — signals still running.
- **TP Hit** — signals that have reached a take-profit level.
- **Closed Win** — signals that closed in profit.
- **Closed Loss** — signals that hit stop loss.

## Sorting

- **Recently Added** — newest saves first.
- **P&L: High → Low** / **Low → High** — by current profit/loss.
- **Pair: A → Z** — alphabetical.

## The table

A dense table with expandable rows. Each row shows the pair, its status, and current
P&L versus entry, updated with live prices. Expand a row for more detail.

## How to use it

Use the Watchlist to keep an eye on the specific calls you care about without scanning
the whole signals list — filter by **Open** to see what's still active, or sort by
**P&L** to see your best and worst tracked signals at a glance.

## Note

The Watchlist tracks signals you've saved. It is not financial advice; trading
decisions are your own.

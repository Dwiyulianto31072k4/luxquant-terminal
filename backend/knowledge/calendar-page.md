# Guide: Calendar (Macro Calendar)

The Calendar page lists upcoming economic and crypto events that can move markets — so
you can be aware of high-impact dates before they hit. This guide explains it.

> Note: This is scheduling/context information, not a recommendation to buy or sell.

## Event tabs

- **All Events** — everything.
- **Macro** — economic releases (CPI, FOMC, rate decisions, jobs data, etc.).
- **Token Unlocks** — scheduled unlocks where locked tokens enter circulation (can add
  sell pressure).
- **Crypto Events** — crypto-specific events (upgrades, mainnets, conferences).

## Impact levels

Each event has an impact rating: **High, Medium, Low, or Holiday**. High-impact events
(like CPI or FOMC) tend to cause the biggest volatility — many traders reduce risk
around them.

## How to use it

Scan for **High-impact Macro** events and **Token Unlocks** on coins you follow.
Around those times, volatility rises and the market can whipsaw — plan position sizing
and stops accordingly.

## Important note

The Calendar tells you when notable events occur. It is not financial advice; how you
trade around them is your decision.

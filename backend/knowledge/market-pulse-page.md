# Guide: Pulse (Market Pulse)

The Pulse page is a real-time feed of market activity — where coins are suddenly
moving, spiking in volume, or getting unusual attention right now. This guide explains
it.

> Note: This is market activity information, not a recommendation to buy or sell.

## What it shows

- **Live activity feed** — a stream of notable coin events (flash moves, volume
  spikes, unusual activity) as they happen. You can filter by source and timeframe.
- **Top Movers** — the biggest movers over a chosen period (e.g. 1h). Includes "flash
  moves" — sharp, sudden price jumps.
- **Heatmap** — the most active coins, sortable by number of events or by percentage
  move.
- **Hourly stats** — how many unique coins are active in the current window.

## How to use it

Use Pulse to catch what's happening **right now** — coins waking up on volume or
making flash moves — then cross-check with the signals on Potential Trades or the
coin's chart before drawing conclusions. Sudden moves can reverse quickly.

## Important note

Pulse surfaces real-time activity as information; it is not a signal to act and not
financial advice.

# LuxQuant — Stability & Reliability Update

Announcement copy for users. Pick the version that fits the channel.
Tone: calm, confident, and sincerely apologetic — the way OpenAI / Anthropic /
Cloudflare frame a reliability update. Owns the disruption honestly, frames the
work as continuous improvement (not a security scare).

---

## 1) Full version — in-app banner / email / web

**Title:** A note on today's downtime — and our apologies

Hi everyone,

Earlier today, some of you experienced errors and short periods of downtime —
trouble signing in and pages not loading. **We're truly sorry for the disruption
and any frustration it caused.** You rely on LuxQuant, and we hold ourselves to a
higher standard than that.

Here's what happened. As part of our ongoing work to make the platform faster and
more reliable, our team rolled out a series of infrastructure upgrades. During
that process a few issues surfaced that briefly affected sign-in and data
loading. We fix problems at the root rather than patching symptoms — so as we
resolved the first issue, our deeper checks revealed a couple of related areas
that also needed strengthening, and we saw those through as well. Every fix was
tested and verified before moving on.

As of now, **all systems are fully operational and running normally.** Beyond
restoring stability, these changes leave the platform **more resilient than
before** — smoother deployments, faster recovery, and new safeguards to prevent
similar issues from recurring.

Thank you for your patience and for sticking with us. Days like today are part of
building a stronger, more dependable product — and we're committed to earning
your trust and improving LuxQuant every single day.

— The LuxQuant Team

diuji dan diverifikasi sebelum kami lanjut.
